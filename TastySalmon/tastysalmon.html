Center for Healthy Living nutrition student recipe:

Pan-fried salmon with kale slaw

Served with kale zucchini slaw, steamed jasmine rice, and wasabi spiked tartar sauce

  • Ingredients
  • 150 grams salmon (or fish of your choosing)
  • Jasmine rice

  • Ingredients for kale slaw
  • Batch: makes 4-5 servings

  • 1/4 bunch of kale
  • 125 grams of zucchini
  • 25 grams of red onions
  • 38 grams of red peppers
  • 18 grams of carrots
  • 12 grams of garlic
  • 1/4 bunch of parsley
  • 12 ml champagne vinegar
  • 125 ml canola oil
  • 6 ml mustard
  • 6 ml egg yolk
  • Salt and pepper

  • Ingredients for wasabi tartar sauce
  • Batch: makes 4-5 servings

  • 7 grams wasabi
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 250 ml canola oil
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 20 ml pickles
  • Parsley
  • Coriander
  • Green onions
  • 12 grams of shallots
  • 12 grams of garlic
  • 12 ml rice vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  1. Preparation
  2. Prepare your wasabi tartar sauce. Start with an emulsion with the rice vinegar, egg yolks and canola oil in a blender. Add wasabi, lemon juice, pickles, parsley, coriander, green onions, shallots, and garlic. Blend until a smooth consistency.
  3. Prepare your kale slaw. Start with the dressing. Start with the emulsion of the egg yolk, champagne vinegar and canola oil. Add garlic, parsley, mustard, and salt and pepper to taste. Mix zucchini (julienne), red onions (julienne), red pepper (julienne), carrots (julienne), kale (chiffonade), and parsley with the dressing.
  4. Steam jasmine rice.
  5. Sear salmon in a smoking hot non-stick pan with oil. Once the fish is seared for a minute to a minute-and-a-half on both sides, put in the oven at 400 C for six minutes.
  6. Plate your fish on top of the kale slaw with wasabi tartar sauce on the side.
