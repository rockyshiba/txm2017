Distilling the facts

The feeling of thirstiness is actually a symptom of dehydration.

Myth: You must drink eight glasses of water a day to be healthy. Fact: Your recommended water intake is actually dependent on your age, gender, and level of physical activity.

A person can live for a month without food, but only a week without water.

Water breaks down food so your body can absorb its nutrients.
