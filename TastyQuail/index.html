RECIPE: Tasty Quail

Check the recipe

Center for Healthy Living nutrition student recipe:

Organic greens

Quail egg, taggiasca olives, toasted pumpkin seeds, cucumbers, bell peppers, bermuda onions, roma tomatoes, white truffle essence balsamic vinaigrette

  • Ingredients
  • 90 grams Organic greens, loosely packed
  • 1 quail egg
  • 4 tsp pumpkin seeds
  • Cucumbers
  • Bell peppers
  • Red peppers
  • Bermuda onions
  • Tomatoes

  • 5 ml white truffle oil
  • 5 ml balsami vinegar
  • 15 ml olive oil

  1. Prepare your mise en place for the organic greens, cucumbers (julienne), bell peppers (julienne), bermuda onions (julienne), and tomatoes (dice).
  2. Prepare your vinaigrette. Start an emulsion in a blender with the balsamic vinegar and white truffle oil. Once the emulsion starts, add a slow stream of olive oil. The emulsion will slowly come together.
  3. Toss your organic greens with the vinaigrette.
  4. Fry your quail egg sunny-side up in a hot non-stick pan with oil for a minute.
  5. Plate your quail egg on top of the organic greens.
